Lasagna Flatbread

Description

Simple lasagna on a pizza - what could be better?

Ingredients

  • 1 (15oz) container ricotta
  • 1 (8 oz) package, shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 (3 oz) package Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tsp italian seasoning
  • 1 lb sausage
  • 1/2 (26 oz) jar marinara sauce
  • 6 flatbreads

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Combine ricotta cheese, 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, egg, and Italian seasoning in a bowl.
